A deep dive.

Take a closer look into one of my favorite projects.

Digital account portal

Indigo Slate, 2018

Microsoft was having trouble marketing the robust capabilities of the Azure Cloud Computing service. My team delivered a tool that helped sales persons distribute and monitor informative sites that were customized for each potential customer.

What did they get?

The project lasted over 3 months, and included extensive collaboration between designers, developers, and client stakeholders. The final deliverables included 2 web applications and a printed & bound information booklet.

As part of the final client hand-off meeting, we delivered this short video to summarize the robust capabilties of the tools.

Understanding Azure.

Customers received a dynamic experience that was full of informative material in video, print, and interactive formats.

The design of the dashboard was a collapsable grid with filters, providing quick access to the site content sections.

Creating and tracking the sites.

Each sales person could use this tool to build a site customized for individual customers. The sites contained interactive demos, educational videos, instructional manuals, and case studies.

In addition to creating sites, the sellers could keep an eye on user engagement scores, content consumption rates, and modify new and existing sites.

It's in a book!

In addition to the web applications, there was an informative guide book to help the sellers get the most out of tool.
